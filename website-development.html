Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

Simple websites take 4-6 weeks, medium complexity sites 8-12 weeks, and complex e-commerce or custom platforms 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines after understanding your requirements.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

Absolutely. All our websites are built with mobile-first responsive design, ensuring perfect display and functionality across smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Can I update the website content myself?

Yes, we build websites with user-friendly content management systems (WordPress, custom CMS) that allow you to easily update text, images, and pages without technical knowledge. We provide full training.

Do you provide website hosting and maintenance?

Yes, we offer reliable hosting, SSL certificates, regular backups, security updates, performance monitoring, and content updates as part of our comprehensive maintenance packages.
