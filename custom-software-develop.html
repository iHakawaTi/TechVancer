Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How much does custom software development cost?

Costs vary based on complexity, features, and timeline. Simple applications start around $50K, medium complexity $100-250K, and enterprise systems $250K+. We provide detailed estimates after requirements gathering.

How do you ensure the software meets our needs?

Through detailed requirements gathering, iterative development with regular demos, user acceptance testing, and continuous client feedback throughout the agile development process.

Do we own the source code?

Yes, you receive full ownership of the source code and all intellectual property created during the project. We provide comprehensive documentation and knowledge transfer.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern, proven tech stacks including React/Angular for front-end, Node.js/Python/.NET for back-end, PostgreSQL/MongoDB for databases, and AWS/Azure for cloud infrastructure.
