Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does Odoo implementation take?

Typical implementations take 3-6 months depending on the number of modules and customization requirements. We use phased rollouts to minimize business disruption.

Can Odoo integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, Odoo has excellent integration capabilities. We can connect it to your existing CRM, accounting software, e-commerce platforms, and custom applications via APIs.

Is Odoo suitable for our industry?

Odoo is highly versatile and used across manufacturing, retail, services, healthcare, and more. We customize Odoo to match your specific industry requirements and workflows.

What is the total cost of ownership?

Odoo offers excellent TCO with affordable licensing, low infrastructure costs (especially with Odoo Online), and minimal maintenance. We provide transparent pricing including all implementation and support costs.
