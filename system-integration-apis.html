Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does system integration take?

Simple two-system integrations take 4-8 weeks. Complex enterprise integrations with multiple systems and data transformations can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines after assessment.

What integration approach do you recommend?

We recommend API-first integration for modern systems, ESB for complex enterprise scenarios, and iPaaS (Integration Platform as a Service) for cloud-heavy environments. The choice depends on your specific requirements.

How do you handle data security in integrations?

We implement OAuth 2.0 authentication, encrypted data transfer, API gateways, rate limiting, and comprehensive audit logging to ensure secure data exchange between systems.

What happens if one system changes?

We design integration with abstraction layers and versioned APIs to minimize impact from system changes. We also provide ongoing monitoring and maintenance to adapt to system updates.
