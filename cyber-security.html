Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How long does a security assessment take?

A comprehensive security assessment typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on the size and complexity of your infrastructure. We provide a detailed timeline after the initial consultation.

What certifications does your team hold?

Our team holds industry certifications including CISSP, CEH, CISM, and ISO 27001 Lead Auditor. We maintain continuous training to stay current with the latest threats and mitigation techniques.

Do you provide ongoing security monitoring?

Yes, we offer 24/7 Security Operations Center (SOC) services with real-time threat monitoring, incident response, and regular vulnerability assessments to ensure continuous protection.

Can you help us achieve compliance certifications?

Absolutely. We specialize in helping organizations achieve and maintain compliance with ISO 27001, NIST, GDPR, PCI-DSS, and other regulatory frameworks through gap analysis, implementation, and audit support.
