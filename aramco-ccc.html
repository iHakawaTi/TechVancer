Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What is Aramco CCC certification?

Contractor Compliance Certification (CCC) is a mandatory certification from Saudi Aramco for contractors working on Aramco projects. It ensures contractors meet quality, safety, and operational standards.

How long does the certification process take?

With our guidance, most companies achieve certification within 3-6 months. The timeline depends on your current compliance level and how quickly you can implement required systems and processes.

What are the main CCC requirements?

Key requirements include quality management systems (ISO 9001), safety management, documented procedures, staff training, audit readiness, and continuous improvement processes.

Do you provide support during the audit?

Yes, we provide comprehensive audit support including pre-audit preparation, mock audits, documentation review, and on-site assistance during the actual certification audit.
