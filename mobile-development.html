Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

Should I build native or cross-platform?

It depends on your requirements. Native (Swift/Kotlin) offers best performance and platform features. Cross-platform (React Native/Flutter) enables faster development and code sharing. We help you choose based on your specific needs.

How long does it take to build a mobile app?

Simple apps take 8-12 weeks, medium complexity apps 3-6 months, and complex enterprise apps 6-12 months. We use agile sprints to deliver working features progressively.

Do you handle app store submissions?

Yes, we manage the entire app store process including preparing assets, writing descriptions, handling submissions, and implementing ASO (App Store Optimization) best practices.

What happens after the app launches?

We provide ongoing support including bug fixes, OS updates, performance monitoring, feature enhancements, and analytics tracking to continuously improve your app.
